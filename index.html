What is CSS?

CSS stands for Cascading Style Sheets

CSS describes how HTML elements are to be displayed on screen, paper, or in other media

CSS saves a lot of work. It can control the layout of multiple web pages all at once

The downsides of computer, smartphone and coffee

Computer:

Repetitive strain injuries: Spending long hours hunched over a computer can lead to pain and discomfort in your neck, back, and wrists.
Eye strain: Staring at a screen for extended periods can cause eye fatigue, dryness, and headaches.
Sleep disruption: The blue light emitted by computer screens can interfere with your sleep cycle, making it harder to fall asleep and stay asleep.
Social isolation: While computers can connect you to others, overuse can lead to neglecting in-person interactions and social isolation.

Smartphone:

Addiction: Smartphones are designed to be addictive, with constant notifications and social media updates that keep you glued to the screen.
Distraction: Smartphones can be a major distraction, making it difficult to focus on work, school, or even conversations with others.
Neck and back pain: Looking down at your phone for long periods can strain your neck and back muscles.
Sleep disruption: Similar to computers, the blue light emitted by smartphones can disrupt your sleep.

Coffee:

Anxiety and insomnia: Coffee is a stimulant, and too much can lead to anxiety, restlessness, and difficulty sleeping.
Digestive issues: Coffee can irritate the stomach and intestines, leading to heartburn, indigestion, and diarrhea.
Dehydration: Coffee is a diuretic, which means it increases urination and can lead to dehydration if you don't drink enough fluids.
Stained teeth: Coffee can stain your teeth over time.
